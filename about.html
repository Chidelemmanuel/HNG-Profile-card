Bio

I am a passionate developer with a love for creating intuitive and beautiful user experiences. My journey into web development started with a simple curiosity about how websites work, and it has since grown into a full-fledged passion. I thrive on learning new technologies and applying them to solve real-world problems.

Goals in this Program

  • To deepen my understanding of modern web development frameworks like Next.js and React.
  • To master the art of building accessible and responsive user interfaces.
  • To gain hands-on experience with backend technologies and AI integration.
  • To collaborate with peers and learn from their experiences and perspectives.

Areas of Low Confidence

Advanced state management, complex backend integrations, and optimizing for large-scale performance are topics I'm eager to get more comfortable with. I believe that consistent practice and tackling challenging projects are the keys to building confidence in these areas.
